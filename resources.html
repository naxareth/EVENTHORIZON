Frequently Asked Questions

1. Why was this website created?
Event Horizon, a centralized online platform, aims to serve as a comprehensive digital hub for information dissemination, community engagement, and content sharing. It provides users with news updates, articles, event listings, and interactive features. The platform promotes events, facilitates community engagement, and encourages content contributions. The creators likely aimed to provide users with a valuable online resource for staying informed and connected with relevant information and events.
2. Who are the people behind this event website?
Nexus Collective: Denulan, Ace Philip S. Escaño, Justin Paul Louise C. Sandino, Shierwin Carl T. Fernandez, Francis B. Vinuya, Russel Vinz A.
3. Does this website includes events outside PHINMA-UPang?
Event Horizon is a digital hub that offers a variety of content beyond PHINMA University of Pangasinan (UPang). It includes news updates, articles, event listings, and interactive features, catering to a broader audience beyond UPang. The platform's interactive event calendar allows users to explore upcoming events and set reminders, and encourages engagement from a diverse user base. Despite PHINMA-UPang events, Event Horizon's scope extends beyond the university.
4. How to register for an event?
The registration process for an event on Event Horizon involves finding the event, reviewing its details, following the registration process, filling out a registration form, and submitting the form. If a registration form is required, provide your information. If a form is required, review and submit the information. If a confirmation email is sent, you may receive additional details about the event. On the day of the event, attend at the specified time and location. The registration process may vary depending on the event and the platform hosting it.
5. Is there a fee to be able to join?
Event Horizon does not specify if there is a fee for joining or registering for events, but organizers may set specific policies. Some events may be free, while others may require registration or ticket price. To determine if there is a fee, review event details, contact the organizer directly, or check the event's official website or contact the organizer through other means.
6. Is there a dress code?
Event Horizon's dress code depends on the event organizers' specific requirements and preferences. It can be found in event details or registration materials, with instructions like "business casual" or "formal attire" provided. If no dress code is specified, attendees can choose attire based on personal preference, cultural norms, and event nature. If unsure, attendees can contact the organizer for clarification and guidance on event logistics, including dress code expectations.
